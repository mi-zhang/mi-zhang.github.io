Teaching
CSE 5469
Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT)
Autumn 2022
This course combines artificial intelligence (AI) with Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to explore the cutting edge of smart systems. Students will learn to design and implement intelligent IoT solutions, covering topics such as edge computing, machine learning on embedded systems, sensor fusion, and real-time data analytics.
CSE 3461
Computer Networking and Internet Technologies
Spring 2024
This course explores the fundamentals of computer networks and modern internet technologies. Students will study network architectures, TCP/IP protocols, and internet applications. The course covers wireless communication systems, network security principles, and emerging networking technologies. Through practical assignments, students will gain hands-on experience with network programming and protocol implementation.
